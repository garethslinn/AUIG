Guideline Icons

These icons are used to emphasise specific guidelines, highlighting their importance through both color and visual symbols.

Caution
A visible warning that caution should be taken.
Avoid
Marks practices or elements that should be excluded or minimised.
Priority
Indicates high-priority information that requires immediate attention.
Information
Provides additional details or context related to the content.
Consideration
Highlights points that need careful thought or evaluation.
